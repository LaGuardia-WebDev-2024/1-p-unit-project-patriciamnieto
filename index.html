⭐️ All About Churro⭐️

All you need to know about the sweet, yet sassy Maltese Shihtzu

  • Churro loves to sing! Every time that World Burn from Mean Girls is played, he has to sing along.
  • He likes being on top of weird places, like the kitchen counter and the top of the car.
  • He really enjoys chewing ice! Whenever I open the freezer, he is always on standby, hoping I drop an ice cube.
  • He loves the beach! This summer was his first time :)
